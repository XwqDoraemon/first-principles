Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a "session"?
One session = one complete thinking exercise from start to finish. You can go through all 5 phases (Understand → Deconstruct → Rebuild → Act → Complete) in a single session.
Do credits expire?
No, your credits never expire. Use them whenever you need to think through a problem.
Can I share my sessions?
Yes! Every session generates a shareable mind map that you can send to anyone.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards and PayPal. Payment is processed securely through Stripe.
Is there a refund policy?
Yes, if you're not satisfied with your purchase, contact us within 7 days for a full refund.
