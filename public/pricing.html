Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a "session"?
A session starts when you begin a new conversation and the first AI reply is returned successfully. That session can continue through the full 5-phase flow. Starting a new session costs 2 credits.
Do credits expire?
No, your credits never expire. Use them whenever you need to think through a problem.
What do I get at the end of a session?
Completed sessions include a structured summary of the core problem, key tensions, likely thinking traps, and suggested next steps. The exact output may continue to evolve as the product improves.
What payment methods do you accept?
We currently support PayPal checkout, including PayPal balance, linked bank accounts, and cards supported by PayPal.
Is there a refund policy?
Yes. Unused purchased credits are generally eligible for a refund within 7 days of purchase. If credits have already been used, refunds are usually not available unless there was a billing error or a product issue. Email xuewq983@gmail.com and include your account email, payment date, amount, and order details.
