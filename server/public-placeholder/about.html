What is first principles thinking?

When you face a tough decision, your brain takes shortcuts — it assumes, judges, and generalizes based on past experience. First principles thinking means stripping away those assumptions and reasoning from the ground up.

This approach is used by Elon Musk, Jeff Bezos, and top scientists. It's backed by research in behavioral economics, the Socratic method, and cognitive behavioral therapy.
